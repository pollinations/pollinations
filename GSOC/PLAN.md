## GSOC 2026 Planner Settle for Pollinations.ai 

- Create an Idea list containing around top 5-10 project ideas for Pollinations.ai that align with the goals of Google Summer of Code (GSoC) and the interests of potential contributors.

- Define clear objectives and deliverables for each project idea to ensure they are achievable within the GSoC timeframe.

- Identify potential mentors from the Pollinations.ai community who can guide and support the contributors throughout the GSoC period.

- Draft a timeline for each project idea, outlining key milestones and deadlines to help contributors stay on track.

 - Prepare a mentor and ideas md which shall be connected to the main gsoc page for easy access by potential contributors.

 - Each idea should have a name, brief description, required skills, mentor name, time of delivery, difficulty and expected outcomes to help contributors choose projects that match their expertise and interests.

 - Timeline based mid-evaluations and other logistics shall be pre-planned and executed accordingly. 

 - Website for the gsoc.pollinations.ai has to be made live before the organization application phase begins.

- Promote the GSoC opportunity within the Pollinations.ai community and on relevant platforms to attract a diverse pool of applicants.

- Brand Kit and Pollinations branding guide should be made available for the contributors to maintain consistency in their project presentations.

- Setup the discord contribution channel with the GSOC category for the @gsoc contributor role and the @gsoc mentor role and @gsoc admins for easy communication and support.

- Plan for regular check-ins and feedback sessions between mentors and contributors to ensure progress and address any challenges that arise during the coding phase.

- Prepare a comprehensive FAQ and resource guide for contributors to help them navigate the GSoC process and make the most of their experience with Pollinations.ai.
