# Frequently Asked Questions

---
id: 1
category: General
question: What is Google Summer of Code (GSOC)?
---

Google Summer of Code is a global, online program focused on bringing new contributors into open source software development. GSOC contributors work with an open source organization on a 12+ week programming project under the guidance of mentors. This year marks the 16th year of the program, and it's open to all newcomers of open source that are 18 years and older.
