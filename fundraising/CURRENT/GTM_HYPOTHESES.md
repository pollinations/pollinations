## Hypothesis 1: Roblox/Gaming Distribution

### The Bet
> Games built on Pollinations will convert their players into Pollen purchasers, creating a user-pays flywheel where developers pay nothing and users fund their own AI usage.

### Evidence
- **Paas's Roblox game**: 51M visits, $12K earned
- **7+ Roblox games** already integrated
- **User-pays model** proven: Players buy Pollen to use AI features

### GTM Motion

```
Developer builds game ‚Üí Game goes viral ‚Üí Players want AI features
                                               ‚Üì
                              Players buy Pollen (Robux ‚Üí Pollen)
                                               ‚Üì
                              Developer earns, Pollinations earns
                                               ‚Üì
                              Success attracts more developers
```

### Key Metrics to Track
- Games integrated with user-pays
- Pollen GMV from Roblox
- Conversion rate (players ‚Üí Pollen buyers)
- Revenue per game

### Actions (Q1-Q2 2026)
- [ ] Launch Roblox SDK with user-pays integration
- [ ] Onboard 10+ games with user-pays
- [ ] Document success stories for developer marketing
- [ ] Target: ‚Ç¨80K GMV in Mar 2026

### Risks
- Roblox policy changes
- Low player conversion
- Single game dependency

## Secondary Hypotheses (Lower Priority)

### 4. Enterprise/B2B Sales
- **Bet**: Larger companies will pay for dedicated support, SLAs, custom deployments
- **Status**: Not prioritized until post-Series A
- **Why Later**: Requires sales team, different motion

### 5. Education/Non-Profit Subsidies
- **Bet**: Grants and edu programs will fund free access for schools/NGOs
- **Status**: Opportunistic, not primary
- **Why**: Low revenue, high support cost

### 6. Geographic Expansion
- **Bet**: Localized products (China, LATAM) will unlock new markets
- **Status**: Not prioritized until platform stable
- **Why Later**: Regulatory complexity, localization cost
