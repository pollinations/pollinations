## üéØ The Archetypes (Who we are looking for)

We are looking for a Lead Investor who fits one of these three specific archetypes. Do not waste time on "Generalist B2B SaaS" investors who want to see enterprise contracts today.

### Archetype A: The "Dev Tools" Native
*   **Thesis**: "Developers are the new kingmakers. Bottom-up adoption wins."
*   **What they love**: SDKs, APIs, Open Source, GitHub stars, Developer adoption graphs.
*   **Examples**: Point Nine (Christoph Janz), Boldstart, Heavybit, Seedcamp (Tom Wilson).
*   **The Pitch to them**: "We are the infrastructure layer for the next million developers."

### Archetype B: The "Gen Z Consumer" Believer
*   **Thesis**: "The next big social/creation platform will be AI-native."
*   **What they love**: Viral loops, huge MAU numbers, Discord activity, Creator Economy stats.
*   **Examples**: a16z Consumer (Justine Moore), Cherry Ventures (Consumer team), Creandum.
*   **The Pitch to them**: "We are the Roblox for AI. 83% of Gen Z are creators, and we give them the tools."

### Archetype C: The "AI Infrastructure" Specialist
*   **Thesis**: "Models are commoditizing; value accrues to the aggregation/distribution layer."
*   **What they love**: Routing efficiency, cost structures, model independence, technical moats.
*   **Examples**: Air Street Capital (Nathan Benaich), radical Ventures.
*   **The Pitch to them**: "We are the unified gateway for all models (Flux, Llama, etc.). We own the distribution."
