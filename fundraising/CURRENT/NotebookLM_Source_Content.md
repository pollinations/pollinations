## Slide 2: Problem and Solution
Today, success punishes the creator. Building is easy. AI bills aren't.

The Vibe Coding Era: Anyone can build an AI app in an afternoon. Teenagers are skipping school to vibe code. Millions of new creators who aren't traditional developers.

The Trap: Without a monetization layer, success punishes you. App goes viral, creator owes thousands in compute bills. Every existing platform charges the developer, not the user.

The Result: Creators go broke or shut down before they earn a dollar. Meanwhile, users won't pay $10/month for a calorie tracker — subscription hell kills micro-apps.

Creators bring the idea. We provide the rails. We combine Compute + Distribution + Payouts.

- Multi-Model Access: 38+ models, 14 providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, etc.) via one API.
- Zero Infrastructure: No backend code, no payment setup required.
- Unified Wallet: End users have one Pollen balance that works across every app. 1 Pollen = $1.

## Slide 3: The Engine — Grant-to-Earn
How the loop works:

GRANT (compute credits for creators) then BUILD (creator ships an app) then EARN (end users pay with Pollen) then SPLIT (creator gets paid, we take a cut)

- We subsidize creators to bootstrap supply. End users pay for usage.
- The grant is our customer acquisition cost — it seeds the ecosystem, not a giveaway.
- Think of it like electricity — we power the apps, users pay for the electricity, creators share the revenue.

## Slide 5: Economics and Competitive Position
Users buy Pollen. Creators earn a share. We take a cut.

- 1 Pollen = $1. Simple, transparent, pay-per-use. No subscriptions.
- The Flywheel: More apps lead to more reasons to buy Pollen, more revenue per creator, more creators.
- Where we are: ~$6K MRR, priced at cost to build trust. Revenue share and margin activate Q2 2026.

Three-layer competitive stack (bottom to top):

Bottom — Suppliers: HuggingFace, Replicate, Fal.AI. They host models.
Middle — Aggregators and Tools: OpenRouter (API), Vercel (Hosting). They sell compute to devs.
Top — Distribution and Monetization: Pollinations.AI.

They charge developers. We subsidize creators — and earn when their users pay.
