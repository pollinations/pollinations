# Pollinations.AI — YouTube-of-AI Deck (8 slides)

Anchors the entire pitch on the YouTube analogy, making every slide map to a familiar pattern.

## Slide 1: Cover
Company name, "The Creator Economy for AI Experiences", seed round. One line: YouTube paid creators for videos. We pay creators for AI apps.

## Slide 2: YouTube's Playbook
YouTube didn't make videos. It gave creators a stage, handled hosting, split ad revenue. Result: 50M creators, billions of users, a $300B business. The same playbook is about to repeat — for AI apps.

## Slide 3: The AI Creator Wave
Vibe coding: anyone builds an AI app in an afternoon. ~50M potential creators emerging. But today, success punishes them — viral app means crippling compute bills. No platform gives creators compute, distribution, AND payouts together.

## Slide 4: Our Stage
One API (38+ models, 14 providers), zero infrastructure. Pollen wallet — users pay per use across all apps (1 Pollen = $1). We grant creators compute (our CAC), users pay, creators earn, we take a cut. The full YouTube stack for AI.

## Slide 5: Already Happening
3M MAU, 500+ apps, 500+ new creators/day, 2.4M daily calls. $0 marketing. One Roblox game on our platform: 51M visits, $12K/month user spend. ~$6K MRR at cost, revenue share activates Q2 2026. Open source community shipping code. 17K Discord, 4K GitHub stars.

## Slide 6: Why We Win
Two-sided network: more apps → more users → more creators. Every app is a storefront and a template — open source means they fork and multiply. Competitors (OpenRouter, Fal, Replicate) charge developers. We earn from end users. Different model, different moat.

## Slide 7: Team
Thomas Haferlach (CEO) — Edinburgh AI, Amazon, published researcher, 15 years AI communities. Elliot Fouchy (COO) — ops/finance, ByteDance/Azure partnerships, payments in 195 countries. Community as hidden team. Second-time founders, Antler Berlin.

## Slide 8: Ask
€2.5M seed. 50% compute, 30% engineering, 20% community. Infrastructure done → Economy building → Discovery next. 18 months: 23K+ paying users, cash flow positive.
