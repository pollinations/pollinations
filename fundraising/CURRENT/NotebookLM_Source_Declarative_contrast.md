# Pollinations.AI — Contrast Deck (9 slides)

Uses before/after and us-vs-them contrasts on every slide. Forces the viewer to see the gap.

## Slide 1: Cover
Company name, "The Creator Economy for AI Experiences", seed round. Contrast: "Everyone can build an AI app. No one can make money from one. We fix that."

## Slide 2: Before vs. After
Before: Creator builds app → goes viral → owes thousands in compute → shuts down. After: Creator builds app → goes viral → users pay with Pollen → creator earns. Show both paths visually.

## Slide 3: Us vs. Them
Others charge the developer: OpenRouter, Fal, Replicate, Vercel. We charge the end user. Others sell compute. We sell through creators. Their model punishes success. Ours rewards it.

## Slide 4: The Product
One API (38+ models, 14 providers), zero infrastructure. Pollen wallet — one balance works everywhere, 1 Pollen = $1, pay-per-use. We grant creators compute, users pay, creators earn, we take a cut.

## Slide 5: Traction
3M MAU, 500+ apps, 500+ new creators/day, 2.4M daily calls. $0 marketing — contrast with competitors spending millions on developer marketing. One Roblox game: 51M visits, $12K/month. ~$6K MRR at cost.

## Slide 6: Old Economy vs. New
Old: subscription hell, every app wants $10/month, users churn. New: one Pollen wallet, pay only for what you use, across all apps. Old: creators need backend engineers and payment infra. New: zero infrastructure, ship in an afternoon.

## Slide 7: Market + Timing
~50M potential creators, models commoditizing, value moving to distribution. Contrast: model companies raised billions to compete on benchmarks. The real opportunity is the app layer above — and it's wide open. Roblox 40M experiences, YouTube 50M creators. We're at 1K apps today.

## Slide 8: Team
Thomas Haferlach (CEO) — Edinburgh AI, Amazon, researcher, 15 years AI communities. Elliot Fouchy (COO) — ops/finance, ByteDance/Azure, payments in 195 countries. Two founders + open source community vs. funded competitors with large teams. Second-time founders, Antler Berlin.
