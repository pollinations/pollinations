# Pollinations.AI — Founder-Market Fit Deck (8 slides)

Leads with the team's unique position, then reveals what they built and why it's working.

## Slide 1: Cover + Who We Are
Company name, "The Creator Economy for AI Experiences", seed round. Two founders, 15+ years in AI creative communities, zero marketing, 2.4M daily API requests. We didn't plan a startup — we built a tool our community needed, and it exploded.

## Slide 2: What We Saw
We've been inside AI creative communities for over a decade. The pattern: brilliant creators build amazing things, then get crushed by compute bills. Every platform charges the builder. No one protects the creator. We lived this problem.

## Slide 3: What We Built
One API (38+ models, 14 providers), zero infrastructure needed. Pollen wallet — users pay per use (1 Pollen = $1) across all apps, no subscriptions. We grant creators compute to bootstrap, earn when their users pay. Show the product in action.

## Slide 4: What Happened
3M MAU, 500+ live apps in ~200 countries, 500+ new creators/day. All organic, $0 marketing. Our open source community ships production code, runs support, onboards creators. 17K Discord, 4K GitHub stars. One Roblox game built on our platform: 51M visits, $12K/month.

## Slide 5: Why Now
Vibe coding: anyone builds an AI app in an afternoon. Models commoditizing — value moving to apps. ~50M potential creators emerging. The distribution layer for AI apps doesn't exist. We're building it.

## Slide 6: The Business
Users buy Pollen, spend across apps. More apps → more demand → more creators. ~$6K MRR at cost. Revenue share Q2 2026. Vs. competitors who charge devs (OpenRouter, Fal, Replicate) — we subsidize creators, monetize end users. Two-sided network = moat.

## Slide 7: Team
Thomas Haferlach (CEO) — Edinburgh AI top of class, Amazon engineer, published researcher, 15 years building AI/creative communities. Elliot Fouchy (COO) — ops/finance, cloud partnerships (ByteDance, Azure), payment infra across 195 countries. Second-time founders, Antler Berlin.

## Slide 8: Ask
€2.5M seed. 50% compute/infra, 30% engineering, 20% community. Roadmap: Infrastructure (done) → Economy (now) → Discovery (next). 18 months: 23K+ paying users, revenue share live, cash flow positive.
