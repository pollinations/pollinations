# Pollinations.AI — Market-Opportunity Deck (8 slides)

Leads with the macro shift, positions Pollinations as the inevitable platform for the wave.

## Slide 1: Cover + The Wave
Company name, "The Creator Economy for AI Experiences", seed round. The hook: 50 million people will build AI apps in the next 5 years. Who captures that value?

## Slide 2: The Shift
Value is moving from models to applications. Models are commoditizing fast. The winners won't build the best model — they'll own the distribution layer between creators and users. App stores charge 30% but offer nothing for AI. API providers charge developers but ignore end users. The distribution layer for AI apps doesn't exist yet.

## Slide 3: The Opportunity
~50M potential creators across app stores, Roblox, vibe coding. Benchmarks: Roblox 40M experiences, Shopify millions of storefronts, YouTube 50M creators. Today's creator problem: no monetization layer, success = compute debt. Users stuck in subscription hell for micro-apps.

## Slide 4: Our Position
Pollinations is the platform layer: compute + distribution + payouts. One API (38+ models, 14 providers), zero infrastructure for creators, unified Pollen wallet for users (1 Pollen = $1, pay-per-use). We subsidize creators to bootstrap supply, earn when their users pay. YouTube of AI — we don't compete with creators, we are the stage.

## Slide 5: Traction
Already happening: 3M MAU, 500+ live apps in ~200 countries, 500+ new creators/day, 2.4M daily API calls. $0 marketing — all organic. ~$6K MRR at cost. One creator's Roblox game: 51M visits, $12K/month spend. Revenue share and margin activate Q2 2026.

## Slide 6: Defensibility + GTM
Two-sided network: creators go where users are, users go where apps are. Every app is a distribution channel. Open source means apps fork and multiply — community as growth engine and engineering force. Vs. HuggingFace/Replicate (host models), OpenRouter/Vercel (sell to devs) — we sit above, monetizing end users. 17K Discord, 4K GitHub stars.
