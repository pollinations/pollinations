## Steering Prompt

Present this as a seed-round pitch for Pollinations.AI to potential investors. Structure it as a clear narrative arc:

1. Open with traction — the numbers are impressive, lead with them to earn attention
2. Frame the two-sided problem: creators self-limit because they fear compute bills (the best apps never get built), and users drown in subscription hell (nobody pays $10/month per micro-app)
3. Show how Pollen solves both sides — one wallet, pay per use, across every app
4. Explain the phased build: infrastructure done, economy launching now, discovery next
5. Make the market case — 50M potential creators, comparisons to YouTube/Roblox/Shopify
6. End with the team and the ask

Tone: confident and genuine, technically literate, not salesy. Like two smart founders explaining over coffee what they've built and why it works. Use concrete examples — the Roblox game with 51M visits, the $0 marketing spend, the 500+ apps already live.

Keep it concise. One point at a time. Let the numbers speak.
