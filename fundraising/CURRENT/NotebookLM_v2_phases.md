# Pollinations.AI — Phased Build Deck (8 slides)

The three-phase strategy IS the narrative. Each phase unlocks the next.

## Slide 1: Cover + Where We Are
"The Creator Economy for AI Experiences." We're building the platform in three phases. Phase 1 is done. Phase 2 is live. Here's the plan and why it's working.

## Slide 2: The Gap
Two groups stuck. Creators: vibe coding lets anyone build AI apps, but the fear of compute bills means they self-limit — cap users, avoid expensive features, never share widely. The best apps never get built. Users: drowning in subscriptions — every micro-app wants $10/month. Nobody pays individually for a calorie tracker, a language tutor, a meme bot. Both sides need a platform that handles compute, payments, and discovery.

## Slide 3: Phase 1 — Pipes (DONE)
One API, 38+ models, 14 providers. Zero infrastructure for creators. This is the compute engine — any creator ships an AI app without touching a backend. Result: 500+ apps live, 3M users, 2.4M daily API calls, $0 marketing. The pipes work.

## Slide 4: Phase 2 — Cash Register (NOW)
Pollen wallet — 1 Pollen = $1, pay per use, one balance across every app. No subscriptions. Users control exactly what they spend. Creators earn on every transaction. We take a cut. ~$6K MRR at cost. Revenue share and margin activate Q2 2026. The cash register is turning on.

## Slide 5: Phase 3 — Discovery (NEXT)
App marketplace, curation, cross-app identity. Where users find AI experiences — like browsing YouTube, not searching app stores. Each app is a storefront and a template. Open source means every app forks into more apps. The discovery layer turns 500 apps into 50,000.

## Slide 6: Why This Sequence
Phase 1 proves creators will build. Phase 2 proves users will pay. Phase 3 proves the network compounds. Each phase de-risks the next. Current proof: 500+ new creators/day, one Roblox game with 51M visits and $12K/month spend. ~50M potential creators in the vibe coding wave.
