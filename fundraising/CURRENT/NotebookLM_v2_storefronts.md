# Pollinations.AI — Storefronts Deck (8 slides)

Each app is a storefront. We're Shopify for AI micro-apps.

## Slide 1: Cover + Hook
"The Creator Economy for AI Experiences." Every AI app on our platform is a storefront — creators run the shop, users walk in and pay. 3M users already walking through the door.

## Slide 2: Two Problems, One Platform
Creator side: the fear of compute bills stops creators from building ambitious apps. They self-limit — cap users, avoid features that cost money, never share widely. The best apps never get built because creators can't risk success. User side: 50 AI apps means 50 subscriptions. Nobody pays $10/month for a calorie tracker or a bedtime story generator. Subscription hell kills micro-apps from both ends.

## Slide 3: One Wallet, Every Shop
Pollen (1 = $1) works across every app — one balance, pay per use, no subscriptions. For users: load once, spend everywhere, full control. For creators: instant monetization, zero payment setup. The universal currency of AI micro-apps.

## Slide 4: The Platform Behind the Shops
One API (38+ models, 14 providers), zero backend needed. Each app is a self-contained storefront bringing its own users. Open source means apps are also templates — they fork and spawn more storefronts. Shopify didn't sell products, it powered shops. We don't make apps, we power them.

## Slide 5: Building in Phases
Phase 1 — Pipes (DONE): One API, all models, all providers. The compute engine behind every storefront. Phase 2 — Cash Register (NOW): Pollen payments, creator rev-share. Turning storefronts into businesses. Phase 3 — Mall Directory (NEXT): Marketplace, curation, cross-app identity. How users discover new storefronts.

## Slide 6: Already Working
500+ storefronts live in ~200 countries, 500+ new creators/day, 3M MAU, 2.4M daily API calls. All organic, $0 marketing. One creator's Roblox game: 51M visits, $12K/month user spend. ~$6K MRR at cost. ~50M potential creators across app stores, Roblox, vibe coding.
