# Pollinations.AI — Subscription Hell Deck (8 slides)

Leads with the user problem. Investors feel this one personally.

## Slide 1: Cover + The User Problem
"The Creator Economy for AI Experiences." You have 12 AI subscriptions. You use 3 regularly. You're paying $120/month for tools you barely touch. Now multiply that by a million micro-apps coming from the vibe coding wave.

## Slide 2: Both Sides Are Broken
Users: every AI app wants its own $10/month. Nobody subscribes to a calorie tracker, a bedtime story bot, a meme generator — individually. Subscription fatigue kills discovery. Creators: the fear of compute bills means the best apps never get built. Creators self-limit — cap usage, skip expensive features, never promote their work. They don't go broke, they just never try.

## Slide 3: One Balance, Every App
Pollen — one wallet that works across all AI apps. 1 Pollen = $1, pay only for what you use. No subscriptions, no commitment. For users: like prepaid electricity — top up once, use anywhere. For creators: instant revenue on every interaction, zero payment infra to build.

## Slide 4: How We Got Here
Built in phases. Phase 1 — Infrastructure (DONE): one API, 38+ models, 14 providers. The compute layer. Phase 2 — Economy (NOW): Pollen payments, creator rev-share. Turning usage into revenue. Phase 3 — Discovery (NEXT): marketplace where users find AI apps the way they find YouTube videos — through browsing, curation, and community.

## Slide 5: Traction
3M monthly users, 500+ live apps, 500+ new creators/day, 2.4M daily API calls. $0 marketing — all organic. Proof the model works: one creator's Roblox game has 51M visits, $12K/month in user spend. ~$6K MRR at cost pricing. Revenue share activates Q2 2026.

## Slide 6: Why Now + Market
Vibe coding means anyone builds an AI app in an afternoon — ~50M potential creators. Models commoditizing, value shifting to apps. But no distribution layer exists for AI micro-apps. Benchmarks: Roblox 40M experiences, YouTube 50M creators. We're at 1K apps — early in an exponential curve.
