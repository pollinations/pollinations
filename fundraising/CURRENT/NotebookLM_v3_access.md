# Pollinations.AI — Access Gap Deck (8 slides)

The problem starts before the bill — most creators can't even afford to experiment.

## Slide 1: Cover + Traction
"The Creator Economy for AI Experiences." 3M MAU, 500+ apps, 500+ new creators/day, 2.4M daily API calls, $0 marketing. All organic.

## Slide 2: Three Problems
Creators can't experiment: API credits run out fast, free tiers are tiny, and most aspiring creators hit a paywall before they build anything real. The best ideas die in a playground. Creators can't scale: the few who do build something limit it on purpose — cap users, skip features, avoid sharing — because popularity means a bill they can't pay. Users can't discover: every micro-app wants its own $10/month subscription. Nobody pays individually for a calorie tracker, a story bot, a study tool. Subscription hell kills the long tail of AI apps.

## Slide 3: Unlimited Experimentation
We give creators free daily compute credits — enough to build and iterate, not just toy around. No credit card, no paywall, no expiring trial. When their app gets users, those users pay with Pollen (1 = $1). The creator never sees a bill. Experimentation is free. Scale is funded by users.

## Slide 4: One Wallet, Every App
Pollen works across all apps — one balance, pay per use, no subscriptions. Users load once, spend anywhere. Creators earn on every transaction with zero payment setup. One API, 38+ models, 14 providers, zero backend needed.

## Slide 5: Three Phases
Phase 1 — Infrastructure (DONE): The compute engine. One API, all models. Phase 2 — Economy (NOW): Pollen payments, creator rev-share. Turning experimentation into income. Phase 3 — Discovery (NEXT): Marketplace and curation. Where users find the best AI micro-apps.

## Slide 6: Why Now + Market
AI API costs are high enough to gate creators but low enough for us to subsidize. Vibe coding is creating millions of potential builders who can't access the tools. ~50M potential creators. The gap between "I could build this" and "I can afford to build this" is our opportunity. Benchmarks: Roblox 40M experiences, YouTube 50M creators.
