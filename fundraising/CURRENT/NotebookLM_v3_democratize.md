# Pollinations.AI — Democratize AI Building Deck (8 slides)

AI is "for everyone" — except you need a credit card and a budget to actually build anything.

## Slide 1: Cover + Contradiction
"The Creator Economy for AI Experiences." Everyone says AI is democratized. Try building an app without a credit card. Free tiers last minutes. Real experimentation costs real money. The democratization promise is broken.

## Slide 2: The Real Barriers
Access barrier: OpenAI, Anthropic, Google — all require payment to do anything meaningful. Free tiers are demos, not tools. A teenager with a great idea hits a paywall before finishing a prototype. Scale barrier: the rare creator who powers through can't afford users. Every API call costs them money. They self-limit — cap usage, kill features, never share. Discovery barrier: users face subscription hell. Each micro-app wants $10/month. The long tail of AI apps can't monetize because individual subscriptions don't work for small tools.

## Slide 3: Remove Every Gate
Free daily compute credits — enough to build real apps, not toy demos. 38+ models, 14 providers, one API, zero backend, zero credit card. When the app gets users, users pay with Pollen (1 = $1) per use. Creator earns, we take a cut. The creator never pays. Experimentation is free, scale is user-funded, monetization is built in.

## Slide 4: Building in Phases
Phase 1 — Open the Door (DONE): Free API, all models. 500+ apps built, 3M users, $0 marketing. Phase 2 — Turn On Revenue (NOW): Pollen payments, creator rev-share. Creators earn from day one. Phase 3 — Fill the Room (NEXT): Marketplace, curation. Users discover AI apps like YouTube videos, not buried in app stores.

## Slide 5: Already Working
500+ apps live in ~200 countries, 500+ new creators joining daily, 2.4M daily API calls. All organic. Proof the access model works: one creator-built Roblox game — 51M visits, $12K/month user spend. ~$6K MRR at cost. Revenue share activates Q2 2026.

## Slide 6: Why Now + Market
Vibe coding means anyone can build — but "anyone" still needs API access. Models commoditizing, costs dropping, but every platform still gates behind payment. ~50M potential creators across app stores, Roblox, vibe coding. We're the on-ramp. Benchmarks: YouTube 50M creators (started by removing the hosting cost), Roblox 40M experiences.
