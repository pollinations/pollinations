### Slide 3 — Problem

Today, success punishes the creator.

We're in the vibe coding era — creating an AI app is trivial. Deploying and paying for it is not.

- **Fragmented stack:** separate APIs, billing, docs
- **Paywalled before launch:** Credit card walls before a single user
- **Success = bankruptcy:** More users = higher compute bills, zero revenue
- **No monetization:** Micro-apps are too small to charge users $10/mo.

Nobody combines compute, distribution, and creator payouts in one layer.

## Section 2: The Solution

### Slide 4 — Three layers. One Platform.

**We combine Compute + Distribution + Payouts.**

- **Infrastructure** — 38+ models, 14 providers, one API. Zero backend needed.
- **Economy** — Credits across every app. Creator rev-share. In-app payments.
- **Discovery** — App marketplace, curation, cross-app identity.

*We don't make apps — we host them, distribute them, and pay creators when users show up. Same playbook as YouTube, new medium.*

---

### Slide 5 — How It Works (The Grant-to-Earn Loop)

**GRANT → EARN → SPLIT**

1. **Grant** — Every creator gets free daily compute grants. Zero cost to start. Enough to build and ship a real app.
2. **Earn** — Users buy credits inside the creator's app. Every AI request costs Pollen.
3. **Split** — Pollinations covers compute, takes a platform fee. Revenue grows with creator success.

### Slide 6 — Why Now

- **Collapsing costs** — AI models are cheap enough for anyone to build real products, not just experiments.
- **Rise of vibe coding** — Millions of non-technical people becoming creators. The builder pool is exploding.
- **Value shift to distribution** — Models are commoditizing. The winner isn't who has the best model — it's who owns distribution.
