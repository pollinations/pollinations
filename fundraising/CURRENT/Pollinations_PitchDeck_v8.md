## Today, success punishes the creator.

**Building is easy. AI bills aren't.**

- **The Vibe Coding Era:** Turning an idea into an app now takes an afternoon.
- **The Trap:** If an indie app goes viral, the creator owes thousands in compute bills. Existing platforms charge the developer, not the user.
- **The Result:** Creators go broke or shut down before they earn a dollar.

**We combine Compute + Distribution + Payouts.**

## The Engine: Grant-to-Earn

**GRANT** (Compute credits) → **BUILD** (App) → **EARN** (End users pay) → **SPLIT** (Creator Payout + Compute Cost)

We subsidize creators to bootstrap supply. End users pay for usage.

*The grant is our customer acquisition cost — it seeds the ecosystem, not a giveaway.*
