## Our Experiment: Everything Open Source

- Backend code: open
- Billing code: open  
- Pricing: transparent
- Development discussions: public on Discord

**Inspiration:** Buffer, GitLab - companies practicing radical transparency

**Cognitive benefit:** No mental overhead deciding what to share vs. hide. Default = shared.
