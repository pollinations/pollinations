AI made publishing apps
expensive again.

  • Indie devs always published for near-zero — a side project could reach millions with almost no hosting cost.
  • AI changed that — every user interaction burns compute. You pay before you earn.
  • Success is the trap — costs scale with usage. Revenue doesn't appear until much later.
  • No monetization — most creators have no entity, can't set up Stripe, can't collect $1 at a time.

You either give it away and go broke, or you paywall it and kill growth.
