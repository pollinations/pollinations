## Slide 2 — Problem

### AI made publishing apps expensive again.

| ⚡ Success is the trap | 🔧 Can't charge users | 🔒 Everyone charges the dev |
|---|---|---|
| App goes viral → compute bill explodes. You pay before you earn. | No entity, no Stripe, no way to collect $1. Most creators are 17 or in countries without infra. | OpenRouter, Replicate, fal.ai, Together — all bill the builder. More users = more cost. |

**Give it away and go broke, or paywall it and kill growth.**

## Slide 3 — Solution

### 🪙 We flip the model. End users pay. Creators earn.

**Pollen: 1 credit = $1. One wallet, every app. No entity needed. No Stripe. No backend.**

| 🌱 CREATORS | 👦 USERS | 📺 PLATFORM |
|---|---|---|
| Free daily compute to start. 38+ models, one API. Earn when users pay. | Buy Pollen, use any app. Pay-as-you-go. One account everywhere. | Covers compute. Handles payments. Takes a cut. |

Same playbook as Steam (games), Shopify (stores), Roblox (experiences).
