# Story & Vision V2: The Community Flywheel
**Angle: Community as the core product, not just a feature**

## Story Bullets (~60 sec)

### Opening Hook
- "Our best features were built by teenagers we've never met"

### Origin Story
- Built creative community in Brazil (Voodoohop) for 9 years
- Learned: communities create value exponentially, not linearly
- Applied same principles to AI development

### The Insight
- Traditional approach: Company builds → users consume
- Our approach: Community builds → everyone benefits
- Example: Kid in India built Minecraft integration while we slept

### The Catalyst Moment
- Open-sourced EVERYTHING (backend, billing, pricing)
- Expected copycats, got contributors instead
- 300+ developers now building features we couldn't imagine

### Proof of Concept
- Community-built apps: 1,000+ live
- Community support: 13K Discord answering questions 24/7
- Community R&D: They deployed new models before we knew they existed

## Vision Bullets (~30 sec)

### The Paradigm Shift
- Software development is becoming communal, not corporate
- AI makes everyone a coder → need new economic models
- We're building the "GitHub meets Roblox meets Unity" hybrid

### Network Effects on Steroids
- Every contributor becomes evangelist
- Every app brings its users to the ecosystem
- Every user discovers more apps
- Every discovery creates more builders

### The Moat Nobody Can Copy
- Can't buy community with $10B
- Can't fake 13K people helping each other
- Can't replicate organic documentation in AI training data
- Buffer/GitLab proved radical transparency works

### End State
- Largest AI developer community on earth
- Self-governing, self-improving, self-sustaining
- "Wikipedia for AI apps" - community-owned infrastructure

---

**Key Differentiator**: Community IS the product, everything else is secondary
