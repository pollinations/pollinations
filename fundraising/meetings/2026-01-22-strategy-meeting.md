### Positioning and Narrative Development

**Core Concept: General Purpose Compute (GPC)**
- Pollinations provides compute for broad range of people
- Meeting developers where they are - whether building booking apps or Roblox games
- Solving the "messy developer experience" with fragmented APIs and resources

**Key Value Propositions:**
1. **Instant experimentation** - No signup, just try it immediately
2. **Competitive pricing** - Start with $1
3. **Community support** - 17K Discord, active helping each other
4. **Open source ethos** - Everything transparent, community-driven

**The Broader Context:**
- "AI has become like electricity - needed everywhere"
- Problem: Why doesn't every app have AI? Because it's difficult
- Solution: Pollinations makes it easy

**Analogies Explored:**
- Like the printing press democratization - big companies had printing presses, now everyone can publish
- Providing ability to write, print articles, and place to publish
- Each analogy captures part of the full picture
