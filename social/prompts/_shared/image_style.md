- **Contextual understanding** - Gets nuance and intent

## Visual Styles by Platform

### Instagram - Cozy Pixel Art
- 8-bit aesthetic, chunky pixels
- Studio Ghibli meets retro gaming
- Reference: Stardew Valley, A Short Hike, Unpacking
- Soft gradients behind pixel sprites
- Emotionally warm, nostalgic

### LinkedIn - Pixel Art Infographic
- Same pixel style but TEXT-HEAVY
- Stats, headlines, bullet points visible IN the image
- Self-explanatory without caption
- Clean layouts, clear hierarchy

### Twitter - Meme Energy
- Playful, shareable
- Bold text, reaction-worthy
- Internet culture aware

### Reddit - Nature-Infused Editorial
- Illustrated storybook infographic
- Hand-crafted feel, thick outlines
- Nature motifs: vines, leaves, flowers, pollen
- Cozy tech aesthetic, not corporate
